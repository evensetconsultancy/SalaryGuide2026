A complete, plain-language guide covering India's biggest tax law 
change in 60 years — written specifically for salaried employees.

- Every deduction: 80C, 80D, 80DD, 80DDB, HRA, NPS, Home Loan
- Old Regime vs New Regime — decision framework
- Complete filing checklist before July 31, 2026

**Part B — Planning FY 2026–27 (Under Income Tax Act 2025)**
- TDS transitions from Section 192 → Section 392 from April 1, 2026
- Full section number mapping: old act vs new act
- Standard deduction unchanged: ₹50K (old) / ₹75K (new)
- Complete planning checklist before April 2026
